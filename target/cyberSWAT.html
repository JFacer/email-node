Our Blog

SAP received a notice from the Bundesamt für Sicherheit in der Informationstechnik (“BSI” – the German Federal Cyber Security Authority) indicating that some SAP user accounts may have been potentially compromised because of malware installed by the Emotet botnet.

As a result of our investigation, SAP identified the potentially impacted customers associated with the compromised user accounts. Some of these impacted user accounts belong to ‘indirect’ customers that are managed through your organization as the designated SAP partner.

More details about Emotet can be found on this external website.

It is important to note that we currently have no indication that SAP systems or infrastructure were impacted. At present, our only evidence is that SAP credentials may have been compromised through individual user phishing attempts executed by the botnet.
